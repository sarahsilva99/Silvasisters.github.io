Christmas 2007

We woke up at the crack of dawn to open up our Christmas presents. Our mom wouldn’t let us go down the stairs yet because she had to “check” if Santa left any gifts under the tree. We also had to impatiently wait for dad to get his butt up. As we were waiting, all three of us were just sitting on the stairs. We had on these long pajama gowns, our Grandma bought us, and some fuzzy socks. When we were told it was okay to go down, we sprinted down the stairs. We had never been more spoiled than that christmas. I can’t remember how many times we screamed “WOW!” or “THANK YOU SANTA!”. Truly one of the best christmas’ in my childhood years.

First Red Sox Game

The first baseball game we ever went to was on Father's Day in 2007. My dad’s favorite team is the Red Sox, so we surprised him with tickets. I remember wanting to catch a foul ball so bad that I brought my own baseball mitt to the game. My sisters and I ordered so much junk food. We went all out because we never had this many sweets at home. Going to red sox games became a Father’s Day tradition. It is easily one of my favorite traditions because who doesn’t love ballpark food and a great game to watch with family?

The time when Jen and Michele cracked Sarah’s head open

When we were younger, we used to love having little “sleepovers” in each others rooms. The night would consist of us watching movies all night and eating as much popcorn as we can.

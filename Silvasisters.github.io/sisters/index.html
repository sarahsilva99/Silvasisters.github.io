Sarah Lourdes Silva

First Born Baby!!

My name is Sarah and I’m a 20 year old college student! Growing up, I always struggled to find my own identity. I grew up playing sports because of my dad, but ended up falling in love with the games. I have played soccer ever since I was a little girl and am proud to say that I still do. I think because I was so attached to sports, it kind of set me back with my other interests. I never gave myself a chance to branch out and discover other things. It wasn’t until I got to highschool that I started to take other classes that I was curious about. Freshman year was when I took my first art class. I discovered that I actually had some talent in art and continued to take classes throughout my highschool experience. It wasn’t until my junior year when I finally discovered what I wanted to study in college. That was the year I took my first Graphic design class. I loved that the major was so open and allowed you to be creative. It made me feel content and I was glad that I had found something I was happy to do.
